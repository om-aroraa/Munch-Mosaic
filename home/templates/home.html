Our Specialities

Rating: 5/5

Espresso with Raspberry

This espresso is a brewing coffee in which hot water is forced through finely ground coffee beans under high pressure. The resulting coffee is characterized by its strong, bold flavor and intense aroma served with the additional taste of fine raspberries at our cafe.

Rating: 4/5

Reuben

This sandwich is a North American grilled sandwich composed of veggies, swiss cheese, and thousand island dressing grilled between slices of rye bread.

Rating: 4.5/5

Choco Pudding

Chocolate puddings are a class of desserts in the pudding family with chocolate flavors. Our chocolate puddings are usually made with milk and sugar, flavored with chocolate and vanilla and thickened with a starch such as flour or cornstarch to give it a strong beautiful aroma and taste.
